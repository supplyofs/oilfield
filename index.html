About Us

Supply Oilfield Services LLC specializes in oil reclamation and handling tanks bottoms, production water, and Off Spec oil. We are committed to providing efficient and environmentally responsible solutions while ensuring a safe working environment.

Our Services

  • Oil Reclamation: Recover valuable oil from waste streams.
  • Tank Cleaning: Safe and thorough cleaning of oil tanks.
  • Lagoon Management: Efficient handling and treatment of waste lagoons.
  • Spec Oil Off: Specialized services for off-spec oil management.
  • Water Treatment: In partnership with Remote Group, we offer advanced water treatment solutions.
  • Safety and Environment: We prioritize safety and environmental protection in all our operations.

Equipment Specification

Our state-of-the-art equipment includes the Hiller 3-Phase Decanter for efficient oil-water-solid separation, ATEX separator units, and advanced chemical injection systems.
